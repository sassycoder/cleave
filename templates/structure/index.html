In 2008, Cleave’s association with the Royal Household was acknowledged by the grant of HM The Queen’s Warrant as “Manufacturers of Insignia” and in January 2009, by the further grant of HRH The Prince of Wales’s Warrant as “Medallists”.

CLEAVE has a rich heritage, combining a unique past with modern style to develop highly sought-after works of art. Our jewellers, goldsmiths and silversmiths create beautiful objects in our workshops in Great Britain.

CLEAVE IS A NAME of subtle magic. Counting Heads of State, Palace Offices, Governments, Civil and Military Institutions from more than 40 countries among its clients, alongside some of the most discerning individuals in the World. Cleave’s unique relationships with Governments and Institutions in the creation of Insignia, State Gifts and Objets d’Art bestow a global cachet on the brand. Cleave sets a new standard for exemplary design and superlative, uncompromising execution of bespoke handmade bejewelled works.
